About Us

IIMM Ayodhya

The Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM) has approved a new branch in Ayodhya, the city of Lord Ram, to support the "Ek Bharat Shresth Bharat" initiative. The inauguration will be an illustrious event featuring prominent figures from the healthcare industry, academic institutions, hotel and hospitality sectors, and other industries, along with IIMM dignitaries. This initiative aims to enhance the nation's growth and development. For more information and to get involved, individuals can contact the Branch Executive Members or visit the IIMM website at [www.iimm.org](http://www.iimm.org).

Target Group: Working Professionals in the field of Stores, Purchase, SCM, Finance, Accounts, Logistics, Materials Management etc and students pursuing their UG/PG degree or diploma.

The team of the Indian Institute of Materials Management (IIMM), Ayodhya Branch consists of experienced professionals and academicians who work together to promote excellence in materials management and supply chain education. The branch is led by Mr. Abhay Mehrotra, Chairman, and supported by Mr. Sanjay Dua, Vice Chairman, Mr. Satyendra Srivastava, Honorary Secretary, and Dr. Deep Chand, Treasurer. Dr. Harendra Kumar serves as the Chief Advisor, providing valuable guidance and vision to the branch. In addition, National Councillors and Executive Members like R. A. Yadav, V. V. Chaturvedi, Pradeep Verma, and Adarsh Gautam actively contribute to organizing seminars, workshops, and educational programs.
